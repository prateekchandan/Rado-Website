Rado - The King of Luxury Watches

Rado is a Swiss luxury manufacturer of watches, with headquarters in Lengnau, Switzerland. It is noted for its use of scratch-proof materials, a field in which it is considered a pioneer.Today the company produces about half a million watches a year with a staff of about 470 in total.

We make the watches which defines your status

Innovative design, research and high-tech materials create the distinguishing traits of Rado watches. Materials such as high-tech ceramic, Ceramos™, sapphire crystal and high-tech diamond inspire designs and give Rado watches their unique character. Several of these watches have been awarded internationally recognised design prizes.
